About the Embassy

The Dubai Embassy represents the Government of Dubai internationally. Our mission is to protect the interests of Dubai citizens abroad, enhance diplomatic relations, and provide reliable consular services.

Consular Support

Assistance for Dubai citizens abroad and legal documentation support.

Document Verification

Authentication and legalization of official documents.

Citizen Guidance

Information services and embassy appointments.
